Projects - Central Energy Plant Officer

2013 (in construction)

Client: Places Victoria

The Central Energy Plant in Officer is to supply energy to the new council offices on the other side of the plaza. The CEP faces the new civic plaza and the Officer station. The building is conceived as a communicating box comprised of numerous screens with animated 19th century photos, evoking the recent history of the site as farming land.
