Projects - North Melbourne House

Photos: Sam Reed

This extension to the rear of a 1920’s weatherboard house in North Melbourne explores the tradition of the local 1970’s architecture. The extension connects the existing house to the rear stables. The Perspex shading throws coloured light into the kitchen and dining areas as well as the outside entertaining area.
